Expected value explained simply
If a bet shows +$56 EV that means if you made this exact bet 100 times you would profit $56 on average per bet. It does not mean you win tonight. It means the odds are in your favor.

Be honest
What this cannot do.
It does not watch tape. Raw knockout power from fighters like Pereira or Ngannou is not fully captured in stats. Those fights get flagged low confidence.
It does not know what happened this week. Injuries, weight cuts, camp changes. None of it shows up in historical data. Always check fight news before acting on a pick.
New fighters are a rough guess. Fewer than 5 UFC fights means not enough data. The model says so explicitly on every affected prediction.
A positive edge does not mean you win tonight. It means the math is in your favor over hundreds of identical bets. Any single fight can go wrong.
Not financial advice. Never bet more than you can afford to lose. Positive EV is a long run concept and any single fight can go wrong.
